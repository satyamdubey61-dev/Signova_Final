Our Mission

Bridging the Communication Divide

Signova is dedicated to enhancing accessibility for the deaf and hard-of-hearing community in India. We deploy cutting-edge computer vision and deep learning to translate Indian Sign Language (ISL) gestures into text and voice in real-time.

By fostering seamless communication, we empower individuals, break down societal barriers, and ensure equal participation in education, employment, and public life.
